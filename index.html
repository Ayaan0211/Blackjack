How The Game Works:

-The player starts by pressing Start Game

-The goal is to get an exact value of 21

-The player continues drawing cards until '21' is hit, he cashes out, or the sum goes over 21

-If the player cashes out he receives 50% of his money back

-If the player goes over '21', he loses 50% of his money

-If the player hits '21' he will 5x his money

Cards 2-10 have the corresponding values, while face cards have a value of 10

If a 1 is drawn, players will have the option of choosing a 1 or 11
